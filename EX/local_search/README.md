N-Queens Problem Solver

This program solves the N-Queens problem using a hill climbing algorithm.

Here is an example with N=4:

Initial Board:
Q _ _ _
_ _ _ Q
_ Q _ _
_ _ Q _
Initial Conflicts: 1

and here is the solved Board:

Final Board:
_ Q _ _
_ _ _ Q
Q _ _ _
_ _ Q _
Final Conflicts: 0
